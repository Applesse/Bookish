Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31.03.2023

Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you visit our website and use our services.

Data we collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Your name and email address when you create an account
  • Your IP address and device information
  • Information you provide when posting content, commenting, or reviewing other users' work

How we use your data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services to you, including allowing you to create an account, post content, and interact with other users
  • To communicate with you about your account and our services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To enforce our terms of service and other legal obligations
  • To analyze usage of our website and services

Data sharing and disclosure

We may share your personal data with the following third parties:

  • Service providers that help us operate our website and provide our services
  • Other users of our website when you post content, comment, or review their work
  • Law enforcement or other government entities if required by law or to protect our legal rights

We may also share aggregated or anonymized data that does not identify individual users.

International data transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your home country. By using our website, you consent to the transfer of your personal data to these countries.

Data retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services to you and as required by law. When you delete your account, we will also delete your personal data, except as required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You also have the right to object to the processing of your personal data or to receive a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are happy with any changes to this policy.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or our treatment of your personal data, please contact us via email or through the contact form on our website.

ABOUT US

Bookish is a place for writers and readers to connect and share their passion for storytelling. We believe that everyone has a story to tell, and we're here to help you bring yours to life. Our platform provides a safe and supportive community for writers to upload their novels and receive feedback from readers around the world. Whether you're an aspiring author or a seasoned writer, we invite you to join our community and share your voice with the world.
